隐私政策

最后更新：2026年2月23日

Sekkii（以下简称"本应用"）是一款语音驱动的任务与备忘录应用。本隐私政策说明我们收集哪些信息、如何使用，以及您对个人数据享有的权利。

我们收集的信息

您主动提供的信息

  • 语音录音：使用语音输入功能时，音频在设备本地临时录制，并发送至 Apple 语音识别服务进行转录。您选择保存的音频文件存储在您的 iCloud 或本地设备中。
  • 任务与备忘录内容：您创建的任务、备忘录和提醒事项内容存储在我们的云端数据库（Supabase）中，以实现多设备同步。

自动收集的信息

  • Apple 账户标识符：通过 Apple 登录时，我们收到的是匿名标识符，除非您主动选择共享，否则我们不会获得您的姓名或邮箱地址。
  • 应用设置与偏好：使用系统标准存储保存在本地设备上。

我们不收集的信息

  • 我们不收集您的位置信息
  • 我们不访问您的通讯录
  • 我们不使用第三方统计或广告 SDK
  • 我们不向广告商出售或共享您的个人数据

信息使用方式

数据用途
语音音频转录为文字（处理后丢弃）
任务与备忘录内容在您的设备间同步
Apple 账户标识符身份验证与账户管理
应用设置个性化体验

数据存储与安全

您的任务和备忘录存储在 Supabase 云基础设施中。数据传输过程中使用 TLS 加密，存储时同样加密。您的数据仅与您的匿名 Apple 账户标识符关联，其他用户无法访问。

日历访问权限

如果您授予日历权限，Sekkii 会在系统日历应用中创建专属的"Sekkii"日历。我们仅写入您主动创建的事件，不会读取您现有的日历事件。

数据保留与删除

  • 您可以随时在设置 → 删除账户中删除账户及所有关联数据。
  • 账户删除后，您的数据将在 30 天内从服务器永久移除。
  • 语音音频在转录完成后不会被保留。

儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。如果您认为儿童向我们提供了个人信息，请联系我们。

政策变更

我们可能不时更新本隐私政策。重大变更时，我们将通过应用内通知告知您。在变更后继续使用本应用，即表示您接受更新后的政策。

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Sekkii is a voice-driven task and note-taking app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Voice Recordings: When you use the voice input feature, your audio is temporarily recorded on-device and sent to Apple's Speech Recognition service for transcription. Audio files you choose to save are stored in your iCloud or locally on your device.
  • Tasks and Notes: The content of tasks, notes, and reminders you create is stored in our secure cloud database (Supabase) to enable sync across devices.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Apple Account Identifier: When you sign in with Apple, we receive an anonymized identifier. We do not receive your name or email address unless you explicitly choose to share them.
  • App Settings and Preferences: Stored locally on your device using standard system storage.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not access your contacts
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers

How We Use Your Information

DataPurpose
Voice audioTranscription to text (processed then discarded)
Tasks & notes contentSync across your devices
Apple account identifierAuthentication and account management
App settingsPersonalized experience

Data Storage and Security

Your tasks and notes are stored in Supabase, a secure cloud infrastructure provider. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Your data is associated only with your anonymous Apple account identifier and is not accessible to other users.

Calendar Access

If you grant calendar permission, Sekkii creates a dedicated "Sekkii" calendar in your system calendar app. We only write events you explicitly create — we do not read your existing calendar events.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account.
  • After account deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
  • Voice audio is not retained after transcription.

Children's Privacy

Sekkii is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
