Frequently Asked Questions

How does voice input work?

Hold the microphone button and speak. Sekkii uses Apple's on-device speech recognition to transcribe your voice, then AI automatically categorizes it as a todo or a note.

Does Sekkii work offline?

Voice recording and basic speech recognition work offline. AI-powered intent parsing (smart categorization, date extraction) requires an internet connection.

How do I set up reminders?

Just say it naturally — for example, "remind me to call mom at 3pm tomorrow." AI will automatically set the reminder time. You can also manually edit reminders by tapping on any todo.

How do I switch languages?

Go to Settings and tap the language option. Sekkii supports English and Chinese, and you can switch anytime without restarting the app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the iOS Settings app → tap your Apple ID → Subscriptions → Sekkii → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro features remain active until the end of the billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Sekkii Settings → tap your profile → Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

常见问题

语音输入如何工作？

按住麦克风按钮说话，Sekkii 使用 Apple 设备端语音识别将语音转为文字，然后 AI 自动将内容分类为待办或笔记。

Sekkii 能离线使用吗？

语音录制和基本语音识别可以离线工作。AI 智能解析（自动分类、时间提取）需要网络连接。

如何设置提醒？

用自然语言说出来即可——例如"明天下午3点提醒我打电话给妈妈"，AI 会自动设置提醒时间。你也可以点击任何待办手动编辑提醒。

如何切换语言？

进入设置，点击语言选项。Sekkii 支持中文和英文，切换后无需重启应用。

如何取消订阅？

打开 iOS 设置 → 点击你的 Apple ID → 订阅 → Sekkii → 取消订阅。Pro 功能在当前计费周期结束前仍可使用。

如何删除账户？

进入 Sekkii 设置 → 点击你的头像 → 删除账户。你的所有数据将在 30 天内从服务器永久删除。
